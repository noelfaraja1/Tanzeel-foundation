About the foundation

Tanzeel Foundation is a Non Governmental and Non Profit Organization which aims at helping the less privilege people in the community which include Orphans, Widows, Elderly and Disabled people.

Services Provided

The foundation facilitates different commmunity services include provision of education studies to men and women in separate events also we are teaching islamic knowledge.Further more we provide community services like wedding, janazah(wash,bury), facilitate information for Hajj and Umrah.

Donation

We have recently received waqf land donated for masjid construction purposes in Chamazi Magengeni area.People in this Area lack a nearby mosque to conduct daily prayers and have to walk a long distance in search for a mosque where they can perform Juma’a and other prayers. Our effort to solve this problem can’t take effect unless we have the support of everyone in our community. That’s where you come in! You can help in this pious activity by donating generously and help us in making the completion of this project possible,for your support is key in eradicating this problem for the Chamazi residents. Would you please consider contributing to help this project move a step closer to our goal. Your donation will have a huge impact on our project and help in providing a nearby worshiping place for the Chamazi residents and in turn save much of their productive time.
