Nisomeshe

Nisomeshe charity event is the program deals with the distribution of school facilities to orphans and less privileged children. It happens once a year in either December or January, and the organization collects different school facilities from the community through online fundraising, seeking for donors to donate as well as gifts and sponsorship from different organizations within or outside the country. The organization usually creates slogans that reflect the vision of the organization towards the program of the respective year.

The deputy Mayor of the Dar es salaam City council Hon.Saad Khimji distributing the school equipments to the less privilleged children in Dar es salaam region at 2021.
