Eid Gala

Eid gala is an event that collects people together during Eid for Islamic celebration. It includes business officials of different companies who sell their products and render their services, while games for children and adults are within the venue together with the dawah from different Muslim professionals and special movies that teach the community concerning certain issues in Islam.

Tanzeel members celebrate eid with orphans and other people in Islamic manner at City Garden Resort in Dar es salaam.
